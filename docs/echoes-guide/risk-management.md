### Risk vs Reward Framework

<div class="arena-card">

<h3>‚öñÔ∏è The Risk Equation</h3>
<p>** Expected Value Calculation:**</p>
```
<p>EV = (P(Win) √ó Reward) - (P(Loss) √ó Stake)</p>



<p>Example:**</p>
<p>70% win rate, 2x multiplier</p>
<ul>
<li>EV = (0.7 √ó 2)</li>
<li>(0.3 √ó 1) = 1.4</li>
<li>0.3 = 1.1</li>
</ul>
<p>10% positive expected value</p>
```

<p>** Risk-Adjusted Returns:**</p>
```
<ul>
<li>Sharpe = (Return</li>
<li>Risk</li>
<li>Free Rate) / Volatility</li>
</ul>
<p>Target Sharpe > 1.0 for good risk-adjusted returns</p>
```

<p>**Practical Application:** Only take risks with positive expected value</p>

### Position Sizing Science

<div class="arena-card">

<h3>üìè Mathematical Position Sizing</h3>
<p>** Kelly Criterion (Full):**</p>
```
<p>f = (p √ó b - q) / b</p>



<p>Where:**</p>
<p>f = fraction of capital to bet</p>
<p>p = probability of win</p>
<p>b = net odds received on win</p>
<p>q = probability of loss (1-p)</p>
```

<p>** Kelly for Signals (Conservative):**</p>
```
<p>Signal Size = Kelly √ó 0.25 √ó Bankroll</p>

<p>Why 0.25?</p>
<ul>
<li>Reduces volatility</li>
<li>Accounts for estimation error</li>
<li>Prevents ruin</li>
<li>Smoother growth</li>
```

</ul>
<p>**Practical Sizing Table:| Confidence | Win Rate | Multiplier | Max Size |**</p>
<p>|------------|----------|------------|----------|</p>
<p>| Very High | 80% | 2x | 5% |</p>
<p>| High | 70% | 2x | 3% |</p>
<p>| Medium | 60% | 2x | 2% |</p>

## Advanced Risk Techniques

### Value at Risk (VaR)

<div class="arena-card">

<h3>üìà Statistical Risk Measurement</h3>
<p>** VaR Calculation:**</p>
<p>**"Maximum expected loss at 95% confidence level"**</p>
<p>** Example:**</p>
<ul>
<li>**Portfolio: 10,000 $SIGNAL**</li>
<li>Daily volatility: 5%</li>
<li>95% VaR: 1.65 √ó 5% √ó 10,000 = 825 $SIGNAL</li>

</ul>
<p>** Interpretation:**</p>
<p>**95% of days, won't lose more than 825 $SIGNAL**</p>
<p>** CVaR (Conditional VaR):**</p>
<p>**Expected loss in worst 5% of cases**</p>
<p>Typically 1.5-2x VaR</p>

</div>

### Risk Parity Approach

<div class="arena-card">

<h3>‚öñÔ∏è Equal Risk Contribution</h3>
<p>** Concept:**</p>
<p>**Each position contributes equally to portfolio risk**</p>
<p>** Implementation:**</p>
<p>1. Calculate each position's risk</p>
<p>2. Adjust sizes inversely to risk</p>
<p>3. High risk = smaller position</p>
<p>4. Low risk = larger position</p>



<p>Example:**</p>
<ul>
<li>**Spark signal: 1% position (high risk)**</li>
<li>Orbit signal: 4% position (low risk)</li>
<li>Equal risk contribution</li>

</ul>
</div>
